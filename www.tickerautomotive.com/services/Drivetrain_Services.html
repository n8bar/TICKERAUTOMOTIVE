Drivetrain Services The drivetrain, which is responsible for transferring power from a car's engine to its wheels, is one of your vehicle's most complex mechanisms. It spans the length of your vehicle, from the engine transmission end to the axles. Regular differential maintenance can help you prevent wear, tear, and damage, extending the life of your drivetrain. Drivetrain damage can be a serious problem for your vehicle. Problems with your drivetrain can get worse in a hurry, and if your drivetrain fails on the road, it could put you at serious risk.
